# EasyPoints — 5-Minute Demo Script

> **Total Duration:** 5 minutes | **Presenter:** [Your Name] | **Role:** Team Lead

---

## 0:00–0:30 — Opening Hook (30s)

**Say:**
> "Every person carries at least 5 loyalty cards — Starbucks, airlines, grocery stores. They forget points, lose cards, and miss rewards. EasyPoints solves this. One wallet. All your loyalty programs. Earn and redeem everywhere."

**Show:** Hold up your phone with the app's Home screen visible.

---

## 0:30–1:00 — Onboarding Flow (30s)

**Actions:**
1. Open the app fresh (or sign out first)
2. Swipe through the 3 onboarding slides:
   - **"Your Loyalty Wallet"** — All points in one place
   - **"Connect Your Programs"** — Link Etihad, Share, Emirates Skywards
   - **"Earn & Redeem Everywhere"** — Use at any partner
3. Tap **"Get Started"**

**Say:**
> "A clean onboarding explains our value in 3 swipes."

---

## 1:00–1:45 — Member Experience (45s)

**Actions:**
1. Sign in with `youssef@demo.com` / `demo123`
2. Show the **Home Screen** — EasyPoints balance, linked programs, quick actions
3. Tap **"Link Program"** → Show linking Share or Etihad Guest
4. Go to **Merchants** tab → Browse the directory, tap a merchant card
5. Show the **Merchant Profile** — category, description, offers

**Say:**
> "As a member, you see your unified balance, link external programs, browse local partner merchants, and see active offers — all from one dashboard."

---

## 1:45–2:30 — QR Earn Flow (45s)

**Actions:**
1. On member Home → tap **"Earn Points"**
2. Show the QR code on screen (📱 Screenshot 5)
3. Switch to **Staff view** (use role switcher or second device)
4. Staff taps camera icon → "Scans" the code (or enters session ID)
5. Staff enters amount (e.g., AED 50) → Confirm
6. Show member's balance updated with new points

**Say:**
> "The QR flow is seamless. Member shows QR → staff scans → points credited instantly. No paper cards, no manual entry."

---

## 2:30–3:00 — Redeem Flow (30s)

**Actions:**
1. Member taps **"Redeem"** on Home
2. Show the QR code for redeem
3. Staff scans → enters points to deduct → Confirm
4. Balance decreases

**Say:**
> "Redemption works the same way. Member shows QR, staff confirms, points deducted. Simple and fraud-resistant."

---

## 3:00–3:30 — Offers & Activity (30s)

**Actions:**
1. Go to **Offers** tab → Show active offers (2x points, free item, etc.)
2. Go to **Activity** tab → Show transaction history with date filters
3. Scroll through earn/redeem entries

**Say:**
> "Members see real-time offers and their full transaction history with filtering by date range."

---

## 3:30–4:00 — Staff Dashboard (30s)

**Actions:**
1. Switch to Staff role
2. Show **Staff Home** — today's transactions, quick actions
3. Go to **Stats** tab → Show analytics (points issued, redeemed, active members)
4. Show the chart/daily trend

**Say:**
> "Staff get their own dashboard with daily stats and a full analytics view — total points issued, redeemed, and active member count."

---

## 4:00–4:30 — Admin & Merchant Setup (30s)

**Actions:**
1. Sign in as admin or show the Admin Dashboard
2. Show **Merchant Setup** — create a new merchant
3. Show **Staff Management** — add staff by email
4. Show merchant settings (point rate, business hours)

**Say:**
> "Admins can onboard new merchants, manage staff, and configure point rates — all in the app. No backend access needed."

---

## 4:30–5:00 — Closing & Tech Stack (30s)

**Say:**
> "Built with React Native for both iOS and Android, NestJS for the backend, and MongoDB Atlas for the cloud database. Deployed on Vercel. The entire system — from onboarding to analytics — was built in one hackathon sprint."

> "EasyPoints: One wallet. All your loyalty. Everywhere."

**Show:** Home screen with full balance visible.

## Tips

- **Keep transitions smooth** — don't fumble with navigation
- **Pre-load all screens** — sign in before demo if possible
- **Use iOS simulator or real device** for the clearest UI
- **Have the server running** on Vercel (or locally on port 3000)
- **Practice the QR flow** twice before presenting
